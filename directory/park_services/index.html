Local Information

We try to provide up to date information on weather and road conditions in Reception. We also have a comprehensive range of brochures which you are welcome to browse through. Do not hesitate to ask if you can't find what you need to know. We are happy to assist with all bookings on request.

Visitors

For Health and Safety reasons all visiting friends and family must report to Reception before entering Haast River Motels & Holiday Park. We do not provide casual showers or use of our facilities to unpaid guests.

Room Servicing

Rooms are not serviced daily however fresh towels can be collected from reception. Rooms are serviced on your 3rd day of say between 1pm and 2pm. Please let us know if you would like a sheet change. Daily servicing can be available for an extra cost.

Lost Property

Lost property will be returned upon receipt of a prepaid envelope. All lost property is kept for one month before being donated to charity.

Insects - Mosquitoes and Sandflies

Due to our wetter environment, South Westland is home to the infamous Sandfly (daytime) and Mosquito (night-time). For your comfort, keep your windows and doors shut at night to block out any Mosquitoes. There is fly spray available in the cupboard underneath the kitchen sink for any that should get in. We also sell Tui Bug Balm at Reception.

Park Animals

Chickens - They love your scraps. Put your scraps over the fence. Please, no feeding them in the hanger or campsites if they have escaped into the holiday park!

We also have Sheep on the property. These are behind fences. Please do not go into the fenced area.
