Local Services & Facilities

Banks/Money

Maps

There is an ATM at Haast Junction, available during business hours only.

Groceries

Maps

Haast Food Centre (On the Spot) is located on Pauareka Road in the Haast Township. 2 km to the right out the gate.

Fuel

Maps

Johnston Motors Haast is located opposite the Frontier Bar & Restaurant on Jackson Bay Road. It offers a Caltex Service Station, AA recovery, workshop and groceries. They have an after-hours pump for fuel outside their business hours.

Information Centre

Maps

Haast Visitor Centre and The Department of Conservation is located on the corner of State Highway 6 . There is information on what to see and do while you're in Haast as well as hut, track and conservation information. It is a good wet day activity with loads of information for all ages upstairs in their display area.

Post Office

Maps

The Haast Post Shop is located at Haast Food Centre (On the Spot) located on Pauareka Road in the Haast Township.

Santana Possum & Merino Store

Maps

Located in the Haast Township, Santana has one of the best ranges of New Zealand and locally made gifts, Merino wool and Possum knitwear garments and furs.
